— About Indica

A legacy of engineered consistency, built one layer at a time.

Founded by Mr. Anupam Verma in 2002, Indica Plywood began with a single manufacturing facility in Tambaur, Sitapur. The company expanded with Woodmake Industries (2013) and Ambika Plywood (2018), building an ecosystem rooted in reliability and long-term market trust.

Today Indica operates three dedicated facilities and an in-house resin manufacturing plant supplying every unit — enabling controlled bonding, dependable performance, and consistent product quality across 30 million+ sq.ft. of annual output.
