Refund & Return Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

Indica Plywood manufactures plywood, block boards, and flush doors as bulk industrial products, primarily supplied through dealers, distributors, and project buyers. Because our goods are typically produced or cut to order and shipped in bulk, standard consumer-style returns do not apply.

1. Order confirmation

Orders are confirmed against a written proforma invoice and advance payment (as per commercial terms). Please verify product grade (IS 303 / IS 710 / IS 1659 / IS 2202), thickness, size, quantity, and delivery address before confirming — changes after production begins may not be possible.

2. Damage in transit

If your consignment arrives visibly damaged:

  • Record damage on the transporter’s delivery note before signing
  • Take clear photos of the damage and packaging
  • Notify us within 48 hours at sales@indicaplywood.in

Verified transit damage is replaced or credited against future orders, subject to transporter and insurance clearances.

3. Manufacturing defects

If a product fails to meet the specifications of its stated Indian Standard, notify us in writing within 15 days of receipt with:

  • Batch / invoice number
  • Sample photos or physical sample if requested
  • Test reports (if available)

Verified manufacturing defects are eligible for replacement or credit. Our technical team may inspect the material before final resolution.

4. Non-returnable items

  • Custom-cut, machined, or laminated products
  • Material used, installed, or altered after delivery
  • Damage caused by improper storage, handling, or installation

5. Refund processing

Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method within 10 working days of resolution. Bank processing time may add 3–7 days.

Need to raise a claim? Email sales@indicaplywood.in with your invoice number and photos, or call +91 93054 62120.
