Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

Indica Plywood ("we", "us", "our") respects the privacy of every visitor, dealer, and customer who interacts with our website indicaplywood.com. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

1. Information we collect

When you fill out our enquiry form, contact us by phone, WhatsApp, or email, or apply to become a dealer, we may collect:

  • Your name, business name, phone number, and email address
  • Your city, state, or shipping location
  • Product interest, quantity, and project details you share with us
  • Basic browsing data such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited (via Cloudflare analytics)

2. How we use your information

  • To respond to product enquiries and quote requests
  • To process dealer applications and onboard authorised partners
  • To share product updates, price lists, and technical documents you request
  • To improve our website, catalogue, and customer service

3. Sharing your information

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information. We may share limited details with:

  • Our authorised dealers or logistics partners strictly to fulfil your enquiry or order
  • Government or regulatory authorities when required by law

4. Cookies & analytics

We use minimal cookies to run the website and understand aggregate visitor behaviour. You can disable cookies in your browser at any time; some site features may work with reduced functionality.

5. Data security

Your data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored on secured servers. While we take reasonable measures, no internet transmission is 100% secure — please share only information you are comfortable sending.

6. Your rights

You may request that we correct, update, or delete the personal information we hold about you by writing to sales@indicaplywood.in. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The revised version will always be available on this page with the "Last updated" date.
